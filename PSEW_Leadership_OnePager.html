Prototype • Simulated data only
Human‑in‑the‑loop
Data minimization (no PII)

Purpose

Provide an early‑warning view of potential public‑safety risks using multi‑sensor fusion (radar, RF, traffic, acoustic), with conservative thresholds and non‑lethal playbooks. The system’s design prioritizes civilian protection, transparency, and auditability.

What it does

  • Fuses anonymized signals into a latent risk score per site, updated each second.
  • Applies hysteresis to stabilize alerts and avoid “flapping”.
  • Enforces multi‑sensor confirmation and human authorization for any RED escalation.
  • Tracks quality metrics (Pd, Pfa, Continuity) and logs every state change with reasons.
  • Shows map layers for sites, safety zones, corridors, and critical assets.

RAG States & Playbooks

GREEN Routine monitoring   AMBER Verify with second sensor; elevate comms   RED Confirm multi‑source; notify liaison; safety perimeter; medical standby; human authorization required.

Verification KPIs

Recall (Pd), False Alarms (Pfa), Continuity

Targets can be set (e.g., Pd ≥ 90%, Pfa ≤ 10⁻⁶) and reviewed in Oversight.

Governance

Oversight & SOPs

Immutable audit log, quarterly reviews, bias checks, strict retention and access control.

Success Criteria

  • ≥90% recall on verified incidents at controlled false‑alarm rates.
  • <10 min median acknowledgement on RED events.
  • Zero confirmed harm from false positives; documented community engagement.

Demo Notes

  • All signals are simulated. No sensitive data is ingested.
  • Configurable thresholds/weights via config.json or UI.
  • Audit CSV & oversight packet (JSON) exportable from the app.
