PSEW — Public‑Safety Early‑Warning

PrototypeSimulated dataHuman‑in‑the‑loop

Purpose

Provide early‑warning visibility of public‑safety risks via multi‑sensor fusion, with cautious thresholds and non‑lethal playbooks.

What it does

  • Fuses anonymized signals into a per‑site risk score.
  • Uses hysteresis to stabilize alerts.
  • Requires multi‑sensor confirmation and human authorization for RED.
  • Tracks KPIs (Pd, Pfa, Continuity) and keeps an audit log.
  • Map layers for sites, zones, corridors, assets.

RAG & Playbooks

  • GREEN: Routine monitoring
  • AMBER: Verify with a second sensor; communications
  • RED: Confirm multi‑source; notify; perimeter; medical; human authorization

Success Criteria

  • Pd ≥ 90% with controlled Pfa
  • <10 min to acknowledge RED
  • No confirmed harm from false positives

Demo Notes

  • Simulated data; no PII.
  • Thresholds/weights configurable (UI or config.json).
  • Audit CSV and oversight packet (JSON) exportable.
